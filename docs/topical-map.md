## Strategic notes

**The Evidence Ledger is the site's defining format.** Every compound profile opens with it. The ledger shows: Preclinical animal | Human pilot (N=X) | Phase X | Approved for Y indication. Colorcoded — strong evidence = green, weak = amber, absent = red. Shareable on Twitter as a screenshot.

**Cross-site canonical strategy:** any "how to reconstitute [compound]" or "injection technique for [compound]" content canonicals to InjectCompass. PepVise owns the evidence; InjectCompass owns the technique; PepTips owns the GLP-1 patient experience. Never duplicate.

**The Reconstitution Calculator is a direct cross-link to InjectCompass's calculator** — same math, research-context framing instead of patient-handout framing. Decision: do we ship it on pepvise.com at all, or canonical to injectcompass.com? Recommendation: canonical to InjectCompass's `/peptide-reconstitution-calculator` with a PepVise wrapper page that frames it research-context. Halves the engineering work + concentrates SEO authority.

**The trending YoY terms (ghk cu, retatrutide, "where to buy peptides") are traffic goldmines at KD 20-40.** Publishing authoritative pillars on these three in Wave 1 captures a wave of emerging search interest that only partial competitors are addressing.
